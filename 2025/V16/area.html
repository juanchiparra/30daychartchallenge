One of the coldest places on Earth: Vostok Station

A Russian research center located in Antarctica, is considered one of the coldest places on the planet. In 1983, the Vostok Station recorded the lowest air temperature ever measured on the Earth's surface, reaching -89.2 째C. The station's annual average temperature is around -54.5 째C, reinforcing its reputation as the coldest place on Earth.
